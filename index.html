About

Spanish Right Away started as a personal project created by Valeria Patiño in Medellin, Colombia in 2018. After spending 4 years teaching the language as a private tutor, the desire to create an online community of Spanish classes for expats, travelers, digital-nomads, tourists, and curious-minded fellow human beings.

We focus on creating and providing diverse content for the different types of learners. We on understanding our student's personal educational goals and helping to acchieve them.
